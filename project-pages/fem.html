    • FEM

    For my advanced computer graphics class I designed a program to take in a list of vertices and tet indices corresponding to vertices and simulate deformation and collisions of objects. I designed and chose data structures to store information about forces at play for each vertex in the object. Using green's strain, stress, and per node forces, I was able to simulate collisions between objects as well as the movement of each vertex depending on the forces at play.

    The two example videos below show a sphere falling due to gravity onto a surface, and a cube falling and colliding with a stationary sphere.
