    • High Dynamic Range

    For this project, I used the Debevec and Malik 1997 paper to construct the high dynamic range radiance map, specifically using section 2.2. I then referenced the Durand 2002 paper on local tone mapping to complete the local tone mapping function. I also extended my function to work with linear RAW images.

    Here are three sets of exposure images and the resulting Durand tone mapped result image!
