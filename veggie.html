    • Veggie Tales Rendering

    As the final project for my computer graphics class my group and I created a rendered scene using OpenGL and C++. I focused mainly on creating crepuscular rays, a skybox, and adding textures to the procedurally generated terrain. The crepuscular rays included adding three render passes, the first for rendering the sun and occluded shapes, the second for rendering the shapes to the default fbo, and the third to render the shapes with rays from the sun casted. The skybox included creating a cubemap, then texturing each side of the cubemap.
